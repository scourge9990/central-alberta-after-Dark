Frequently Asked Questions

How do I verify my email?

After signing up, check your inbox for a verification email. If you don't see it, check your spam folder or use the "Resend Verification Email" option on the login page.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

Go to Settings and click "Cancel Subscription", or email us at support@centralalbertaafterdark.com.

How do I delete my account?

Contact us at support@centralalbertaafterdark.com with your account details and we'll help you delete your account and personal data.

How can I advertise on the site?

Email us at centralalbertaafterdarkads@gmail.com for advertising rates and information.
