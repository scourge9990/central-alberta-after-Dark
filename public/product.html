About Us

Central Alberta After Dark was created to solve a common problem: night owl workers often struggle to maintain social connections because their schedules don't align with traditional social activities and businesses.

Whether you're a nurse working night shifts, a trucker on long hauls, a factory worker on rotating schedules, or any other night owl — you deserve a community that understands your lifestyle.

Our platform is designed for the unique needs of night owls. Connect with people who won't judge you for missing events, who celebrate your weird sleep schedule, and who might even want to grab coffee at 8am after your night shift.
